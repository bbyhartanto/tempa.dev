Animation becomes expensive when it exists only to prove that animation exists.

## Strong motion design usually does three things

- Guides attention to the right decision at the right moment
- Makes transitions feel expected instead of abrupt
- Reinforces the tone of the brand without overwhelming the interface

## A better standard

If motion makes the experience harder to use on mobile, it is not premium. It is just heavy.
