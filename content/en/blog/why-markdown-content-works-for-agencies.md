For many agency teams, a heavyweight CMS is unnecessary during the early stage.

## Why markdown helps

- Content lives with the codebase
- Writing changes are reviewable in Git
- Collections make content queryable like structured data

## The tradeoff

Editors need some familiarity with frontmatter and repository workflows unless you later add a visual CMS layer.
