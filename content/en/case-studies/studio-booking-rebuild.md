The original experience asked users to make too many decisions too early. Drop-off happened before intent had a chance to build.

## What changed

- Reorganized the flow around customer confidence instead of internal process order
- Reduced unnecessary fields in the first step
- Introduced clearer package hierarchy and trust cues

## Result

The experience felt shorter, more premium, and easier to commit to.
