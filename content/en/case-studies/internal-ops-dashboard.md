Before the redesign, teams were relying on chat, sheets, and ad-hoc approvals. Visibility was low and follow-up work was high.

## Focus

- Make request intake consistent
- Clarify ownership and next actions
- Reduce manual coordination

## Outcome

A calmer internal process with better visibility and fewer repetitive clarifications.
